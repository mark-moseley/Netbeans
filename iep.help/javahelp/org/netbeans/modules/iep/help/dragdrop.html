Dragging and Dropping IEP Operators

See Also

You specify and connect IEP operators by dragging and dropping them. To do so:

  1. Place your cursor on an operator on the IEP Palette.
  2. Drag the operator from the Palette onto the canvas.
  3. Add another operator from the Palette to the canvas.
  4. Connect one port on one operator to a port on the connecting operator by dragging an arrow from the first operator to the second.
