Adding IEP Operators to an IEP Process

See Also

You specify and connect IEP operators by dragging them from the Palette to the Design view. To do so:

  1. Place your cursor on an operator on the IEP Palette.
  2. Drag the operator from the Palette onto the Design view.
  3. Add another operator from the Palette to the Design view.
  4. Connect one port on one operator to a port on the connecting operator by dragging an arrow from the first operator to the second.
  5. <move to edit> Once you have created and connected all operators for your IEP process, you can click the validation icon at the top of the IEP Design view to validate the operators that make up your IEP process. The validation process runs a set of predefined validation rules, and provides output in the IDE's output area. For example, one validation rule checks to see if all required operator properties have been set, and reports if the property has not been set.
