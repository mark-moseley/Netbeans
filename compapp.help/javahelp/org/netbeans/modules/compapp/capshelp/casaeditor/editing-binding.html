Adding and Configuring WSDL Ports

See Also

Use the CASA Editor to add and configure the WSDL ports that represent the Binding Components of a composite application.

To add and configure a WSDL Port:

  1. From the Palette, select the icon for the WSDL Binding you want to add.
    The Palette displays all WSDL bindings that are available from the GlassFish Application Server.
  2. Drag the icon for the binding to the WSDL Ports section of the Design Area.
    A representation of the binding appears in the WSDL Ports section.
    Note: You can also right-click in the WSDL Ports section of the Design Area to specify a WSDL binding.
  3. Connect the WSDL Binding to a JBI module or another WSDL binding by doing the following:
    • Drag a Consume Endpoint on one component to a Provide Endpoint on another component.
    Refer to Connecting Composite Application Components for more information on connecting a WSDL Binding to other components.
  4. Select the Edit icon on the WSDL Binding to view or edit its properties.
    You can also use the Navigator window for advanced editing of the composite application.
