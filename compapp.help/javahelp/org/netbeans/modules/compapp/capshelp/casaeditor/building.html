CASA Editor: Building a BPEL Project

See Also

To build a BPEL project:

  1. From the Projects window, click on the BPEL project and drag it to the JBI module area.
  2. Click the build button on the CASA toolbar to build the BPEL project.

Now you are ready to create connections(link here) between a WSDL port and your BPEL project.
