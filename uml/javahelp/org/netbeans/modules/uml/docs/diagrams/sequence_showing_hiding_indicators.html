Showing and Hiding Indicators

To show or hide the spacing indicator between any two lifelines, in the gray bar at the top of the Sequence diagram, right-click, and then choose Show Indicators in Track Bar.
If the spacing indicator was hidden, it is now shown, and the Show Indicators in Track Bar menu item displays a check mark. If the spacing indicator was shown, it is now hidden, and the Show Indicators in Track Bar menu item displays no check mark.

  • To re-order Lifelines, simply drag a timeline to a new location.
