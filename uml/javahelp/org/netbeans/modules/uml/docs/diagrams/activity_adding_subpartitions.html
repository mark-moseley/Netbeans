Adding Subpartitions

See Also

You can add Subpartitions to the right (columns) or to the bottom (rows) of a Partition in an Activity diagram.

  • You can only expand Partitions in one direction: horizontally (to the right with columns) or vertically (to the bottom with rows).

To expand a Partition horizontally:

  • In the Diagram editor, right-click a Partition and choose Partitions > Add Partition Column to Right.

To expand a Partition vertically:

  • In the Diagram editor, right-click a Partition and choose Partitions > Add Partition Row to Bottom.
