Nesting Components Inside Nodes

You can nest Components inside Nodes by dropping or moving the component into a Node. Do one of the following:

To nest components inside nodes:

  1. In the Modeling Palette, click the Component icon , and then drop onto the target node.
  2. In the target Diagram window, click one of the target components, and then drag onto the target node.
    The IDE nests the component inside the node.
