Specifying the Retain Minimum Width Spacing Restriction

When you want to restrict the mobility of any two Sequence diagram lifelines such that they can be moved no closer to each other, you can specify a minimum width space between the target lifelines by doing the following:

  • At the top of the Sequence diagram, between the target lifelines, right-click the gray bar, and then choose Retain This Minimum Width. Make sure you have selected the two lifelines.
    The IDE applies the restriction, and displays between the target lifelines a straight line with straight, vertical ends.
  • To re-order Lifelines, simply drag a timeline to a new location.
