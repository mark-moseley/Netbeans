UML Design Center

See Also

The UML Design Center serves as a storage area for design patterns that solve common design and production problems in your UML Modeling projects. You can create, view, and apply design patterns to existing elements in the Projects window, or you can create new elements to provide a solution to a specific problem.

UML Modeling patterns are stored in the Design Center Catalog, from the project level downward, for convenient reuse.

To display the UML Design Center window:

  • Choose Window > Other > UML Design Center.
    The UML Design Center window shows the elements contained in your local project.

To display the contents of the Design Pattern Catalog:

  • In the UML Design Center window, expand the Design Center node and then the Design Pattern Catalog node.

The catalog contains standard pattern projects, and it can also contain user-defined projects in which you create your own patterns.
