Navigating by Links in Diagrams

See Also

The IDE lets you navigate diagram components by their connecting links. This method of diagram navigation makes either the link's originating or destination component the active component, and centers that component in the middle of the Diagram window. You can use this navigation method only by right-clicking the target link.

To use navigation links in diagrams:

  1. In the UML Diagrams window, select a link.
  2. Right-click the link, and, under Find, do one of the following:
    • To locate the link's destination component, choose Target Element.
    • To locate the link's originating component, choose Source Element.
    The IDE displays the component, centers the Diagram window on that component, and makes it the active component.
