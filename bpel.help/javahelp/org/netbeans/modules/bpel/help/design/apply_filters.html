BPEL Designer: Applying Filters To Diagram Elements

You can apply filters to either show or hide the Partner Link and Sequence elements on your business process diagram. You do this by clicking the toggle buttons on the Editor toolbar in the Design view. By default, these filters are disabled and you can see all elements on the diagram.

To hide Partner Link elements:

  • Click the Show Partner Links button on the editor toolbar in the Design view.

To hide Sequence elements:

  • Click the Show Sequences button on the editor toolbar in the Design view.
  • You cannot add new PartnerLink or Sequence elements to the diagram if you chose not to show them. You also cannot create or modify message flows
