Deleting a Page Navigation Connector

See Also | Tutorials

In the Page Flow editor, a connector is the line that connects two pages to indicate that this page navigation will be available at runtime. If you delete a connector, the two pages on either side of the connector are no longer connected.

To delete a connector:

  • In the Page Flow editor, right-click the connector and choose Delete.
