Deleting an Enterprise JavaBean Component

See Also

If you do not want to use an Enterprise JavaBean component (enterprise bean) on a particular page, you can delete it from the page. You can also delete set of enterprise beans from the Servers window. Once you delete an enterprise bean set from the Servers window, you can no longer use its enterprise beans in any page.

To delete an enterprise bean from the page:

  • In the Outline window, right-click the enterprise bean and choose Remove.

To delete an enterprise bean from the Servers window:

  • In the Servers window, right-click the enterprise bean and choose Remove.
