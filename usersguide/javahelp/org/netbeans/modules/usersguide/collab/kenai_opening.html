Opening a Kenai Project

See Also

You can open any Kenai project directly in the IDE. You do not need to be logged in to open a project, and you can open project even if you are not a member of that project. After you open a project, a node representing the project is displayed in the Kenai dashboard.

To open a Kenai project:

  • Choose Team > Kenai > Open Kenai Project from the main menu.

    Alternatively, you can click Open project in the Kenai dashboard.
