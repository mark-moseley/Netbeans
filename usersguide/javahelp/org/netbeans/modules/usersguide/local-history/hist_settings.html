Adjusting Local History Settings

See Also

The IDE's local file history support currently enables you to adjust the duration of time for which files are locally stored on your system.

To adjust the local history storage duration:

  1. Choose Tools > Options from the main menu.
  2. Select the Miscellaneous tab along the top of the Options window, then under Versioning Systems in the left column select Local History.
  3. In the corresponding text field, enter an integer for the number of days you want the IDE to retain local histroy versions.
